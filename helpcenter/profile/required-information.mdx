## Overview

The first step after logging into **Polygon One** is to complete your company profile. Providing accurate company information is mandatory for EUDR compliance.

## Mandatory Information

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Company Details" icon="building">
    - Official registered company name
    - Legal form (GmbH, AG, Ltd, etc.)
    - Registration number
  </Card>
  <Card title="Address" icon="location-dot">
    - Street address
    - City and postal code
    - Country
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## EU TRACES Integration

<Info>
If you intend to automate the creation of Due Diligence Statements (DDS) via the EU TRACES API, you must provide your credentials.
</Info>

### Required Credentials

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **EU TRACES Username** | Your registered EU TRACES account username |
| **Login Token** | API token generated from your EU TRACES account |

### Generating API Credentials in EU TRACES

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into EU TRACES">
    Access [EU TRACES](https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/tracesnt) with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to your account settings or API management section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Token">
    Create a new API token for Polygon One integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy Credentials">
    Copy the username and token to enter in Polygon One.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Keep your API token secure. Do not share it publicly or commit it to version control.
</Warning>

## EORI Number

Your **Economic Operators Registration and Identification (EORI)** number is required for customs-related operations:

- Format: Country code + up to 15 characters (e.g., DE123456789012345)
- Used for all EU import/export declarations
- Links your DDS submissions to customs clearance

## Contact Information

Provide a compliance contact for regulatory communications:

- Primary contact name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)

<Tip>
Keep your contact information up to date. National authorities may use this information for compliance inquiries.
</Tip>
