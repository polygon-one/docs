## Overview

Manage your company profile, team members, and system integrations from the **Settings** area.

## Updating Company Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Click your profile icon and select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Company Profile">
    Navigate to **Company Profile** to update your information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Review and save your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Changes to your legal company name or registration number may require re-verification for compliance purposes.
</Warning>

## Team Management

### Adding Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Team">
    Go to **Settings → Team Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite User">
    Click **"Invite"** and enter the user's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Select the appropriate role for the new team member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    The user receives an email invitation to join your organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### User Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access including billing and team management |
| **Manager** | Create and manage DDS, approve supplier data |
| **Analyst** | View compliance data, generate reports |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to dashboards |
