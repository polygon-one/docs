## What is a Due Diligence Statement?

A **Due Diligence Statement (DDS)** is an official declaration submitted to EU TRACES confirming that your products comply with EUDR requirements. It links your verified supply chain data to specific commercial volumes.

## The DDS Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order Ingestion" icon="dolly">
    Sync your purchase or sales orders via API or the flexible CSV Import tool.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create DDS" icon="file-pen">
    Formally declare compliance for specific volumes. Select your activity type (Import or Export), supplier, and articles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order Assignment" icon="link">
    Link real logistics orders to your valid DDS. The system ensures you never exceed the declared DDS volume.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export for Customs" icon="passport">
    Export orders to get the **DDS Reference Number** and **Verification Number** required for customs clearance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Status Tracking

Your DDS will move through the following statuses:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Processing** | The system is preparing the data. |
| **Submitted** | Data has been sent to EU TRACES. |
| **Validated** | EU TRACES has accepted the DDS and issued a Reference Number. |
| **Rejected** | EU TRACES denied the submission (check details for errors). |
