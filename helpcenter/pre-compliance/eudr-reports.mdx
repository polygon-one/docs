## AI Document Validation

<Info>
Our system automatically checks uploaded documents for plausibility and authenticity.
</Info>

Common validation flags:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Country Mismatch" icon="globe">
    The document's origin country doesn't match the declared production location.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Date Inconsistency" icon="calendar">
    Document dates fall outside the expected timeframe for the shipment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Suspected Fake" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    AI has detected anomalies suggesting the document may not be authentic.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Review flagged documents in the **Documents Overview** section.

## Resolving Issues

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Flags">
    Navigate to the flagged article and review the specific issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Supplier">
    Use the **Message Center** to contact suppliers directly through the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request Corrections">
    Ask suppliers to provide corrected or additional documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-validate">
    Once new documents are uploaded, the system automatically re-validates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generating Reports

Once compliant, generate your audit-ready **EUDR Analysis Report**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Articles">
    Choose the compliant articles to include in your report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Report">
    Click **"Generate EUDR Report"** to create the documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download & Store">
    Download the report for your records and regulatory submissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
This report serves as proof for national authorities (like the **BLE** in Germany) that you have performed due diligence and risk mitigation according to EUDR requirements.
</Note>
