## Overview

If you already have the geolocation data or legal certificates (e.g., **FLEGT** licenses), you can bypass the supplier invite and enter the data directly.

## Adding Production Plots

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Map View">
    Navigate to the article requiring compliance data and open the **Map** view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Plot">
    Click the **"+"** icon to begin adding a production plot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Draw or Upload">
    Either draw the plot boundaries manually or upload a geolocation file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Plot">
    Confirm the plot details and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
When drawing plots manually, use the polygon tool for irregular shapes or the point tool for small production areas under 4 hectares.
</Tip>

## Uploading Documents

Upload certificates and evidence directly to the associated article:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Supported Formats" icon="file">
    - PDF documents
    - Images (JPG, PNG)
    - Scanned certificates
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accepted Documents" icon="certificate">
    - FLEGT licenses
    - FSC/PEFC certificates
    - Land ownership titles
    - Harvesting permits
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Completing the Legal Questionnaire

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Questionnaire">
    Navigate to the article's compliance section and select **"Complete Questionnaire"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer Legal Questions">
    Complete the country-specific legal compliance checklist on behalf of your supplier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach Evidence">
    Upload supporting documents for each declaration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for Review">
    Review your answers and submit to finalize the risk assessment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Ensure all uploaded documents are valid and not expired. The AI validation system will flag suspicious or outdated documentation.
</Warning>
