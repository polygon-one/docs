## Overview

To begin your compliance journey, you must import your articles and suppliers into Polygon One. This forms the foundation for all subsequent risk analysis and due diligence processes.

## Import Methods

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Manual Entry" icon="keyboard">
    Best for small updates or testing individual records.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CSV Import" icon="file-csv">
    Ideal for bulk uploading large datasets efficiently.
  </Card>
  <Card title="REST API" icon="code">
    The most efficient method for syncing data directly from your ERP or PIM systems.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Importing Articles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Articles">
    Go to the **Articles** section in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Import Method">
    Select your preferred import method: Manual, CSV, or API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Required Fields">
    Ensure all mandatory fields are mapped correctly, including HS codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Confirm">
    Review the import preview and confirm to complete the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Importing Suppliers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Suppliers">
    Go to the **Suppliers** section in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Supplier Information">
    Include company name, address, and contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link to Articles">
    Associate suppliers with the relevant articles they provide.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## HS Code Analysis

<Info>
Once articles are imported, Polygon One automatically analyzes their **HS Codes** to flag products that fall under EUDR scope.
</Info>

Products containing the following commodities are automatically flagged:
- Cattle
- Cocoa
- Coffee
- Oil palm
- Rubber
- Soya
- Wood
